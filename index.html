Well-known Sayings

1.Life is a pendulum that swings between misery and ennui.

2.No one lives in the past, and no one lives in the future. Reality is the only form that life truly occupies.

3.Ordinary people merely think how they shall spend their time; a man of talent tries to use it.

4.Wealth is like sea water, the more we drink, the thirstier we become. The same is true of fame.

5.It's either lonely or vulgar.
